Chicken Burger

A juicy, tender chicken breast fillet, seasoned to perfection and either grilled or crispy-fried, served on a freshly toasted bun. Topped with crunchy lettuce, ripe tomato slices, creamy mayonnaise, and a hint of tangy mustard or signature sauce. Perfectly balanced in flavor and texture, this burger is a crowd favorite!

Key Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast (grilled or crispy fried)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion (optional)
  • Mayonnaise or special sauce
  • Toasted burger bun

Variations You Can Offer (Optional):

  • Spicy Chicken Burger – With jalapeños and spicy sauce
  • Grilled Chicken Burger – Healthier version with smoky flavor
  • Cheesy Chicken Burger – With a slice of cheddar or mozzarella
